Jotto - The Five Letter Word Game
     
Jotto

Jotto is a fun word game where you have to guess a secret 5-letter word by asking how many letters from other 5 letter words are in the secret word. Note that the secret word may not have repeating characters, but in this version of the game, the words you ask can.

For example, suppose the secret word was phone. If I were to start with the word tooth, then the game would tell me 3 of my letters are in the secret word. This would lead me to belive that the letter h is definitely in the secret word, and furthermore, either t or o as well. Through the power of deduction, one must try to guess the secret word in as few guesses as possible.

Note that at the bottom of the game area there is an alphabet. This should help you keep track of which letters you are certain are part of the word or not.

Jotto was invented by Morton Rosenfeld in 1955. See Wikipedia for more.
